## Master Rudy Timmerman

Master Rudy Timmerman was a prominent Canadian martial artist known for founding **Ancient Fighting Arts** in **Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario**. Originally trained in **Hapkido**, he later affiliated with the **World Kuk Sool Association (WKSA)** and was awarded a **5th Degree Black Belt** by Grandmaster **In Hyuk Suh**. His school was briefly part of WKSA during this period.

However, following differences in direction and philosophy, he left WKSA and re-established the **Kong Shin Bup** organization, reviving its structure and curriculum under his leadership.

He is also notable as one of the first Hapkido instructors in Canada and contributed significantly to martial arts development in Ontario.

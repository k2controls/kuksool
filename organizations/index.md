# Kuk Sool–Related Organizations

This section documents the **organizations, associations, and lineages**
connected to Kuk Sool and related Korean martial arts.  
It includes both **current organizations** and **historically significant
groups** that influenced the development, spread, or evolution of the art.

The goal is **clarity, accuracy, and respectful documentation**, not advocacy.

---

## Current Organizations

### World Kuk Sool Association (WKSA)
- Historically associated with **Kuk Sool Won (국술원)**
- Founded and led for decades by **In Hyuk Suh (서인혁)**
- Centralized curriculum, testing, and international school affiliation
- Recently underwent a major leadership transition

➡️ See:  
- [`WKSA`](./wksa/)

---

### United Mirae Kuk Sool (UMKS)
- Founded by **Sung Jin Suh**
- Emphasizes continuity of Kuk Sool foundations with a forward-looking vision
- “Mirae (미래)” translates to **Future**

➡️ See:  
- [`United Mirae Kuk Sool`](./umks/)

## Historically Significant Organizations

These organizations are important for understanding **lineage, divergence,
and influence**, even if they are no longer active in their original form.

### Kong Shin Bup (공신법)
- Associated with **Pak In Shyuk (박인숙)**
- Later preserved and promoted by **Rudy Timmerman**
- Represents an early strand of Kuk Sool / Hapkido development in North America

### Ancient Fighting Arts
- Founded by **Rudy Timmerman** in Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario (1973)
- Early hub for Korean martial arts training in Canada

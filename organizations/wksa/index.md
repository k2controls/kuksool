# World Kuk Sool Association (WKSA)

## Names and Usage

**Common name (English):** World Kuk Sool Association  
**Abbreviation:** WKSA

WKSA is the organization historically associated with **Kuk Sool Won (국술원)**, the modern system widely taught internationally.

> **국술원 (Kuk Sool Won)**  
> - **국술 (Kuk Sool):** “National Martial Arts” (commonly rendered as “Kuk Sool”)  
> - **원 (Won):** institute / academy / training hall (context-dependent)

---

## Identity and Purpose

WKSA is best known for standardizing and disseminating Kuk Sool Won internationally through:
- formal curriculum structure (technique sets, hyung, weapons, rank requirements)
- centralized testing and ranking norms
- instructor development and school affiliation

## Leadership

**Founder / long-time Grandmaster:** **In Hyuk Suh (서인혁)**

### Recent Leadership Transition (2025–2026)
A major WKSA transition occurred in the 2025–2026 period:
- **In Hyuk Suh** stepped back from active leadership
- **Choon-ok Harmon** and **Barry Harmon** were promoted to **10th Dan**
- leadership was passed to **Alex Suh** (second son)
